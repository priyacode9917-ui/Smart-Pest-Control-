Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Our Answers

How often should pest control be done?

Pest control should generally be done every 3 to 6 months. Regular inspections help prevent infestations and maintain a healthy environment.

Is pest control safe?

Yes. We use government-approved and eco-friendly treatments that are safe for families, children, and pets when used properly.

How long does treatment take?

Most treatments take between 30 minutes and 2 hours depending on the property size and pest problem.

What pests do you remove?

We remove termites, mosquitoes, cockroaches, ants, rodents, bed bugs, spiders, flies, and many other common pests.
