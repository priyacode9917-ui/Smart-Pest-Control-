Types of Pests

Termites


Termites silently destroy wooden furniture, doors, windows and building structures. They are among the most destructive pests.

Mosquitoes


Mosquitoes spread diseases like dengue, malaria and chikungunya. They breed in stagnant water.

Cockroaches


Cockroaches contaminate food and trigger allergies. They hide in dark and moist places.

Rodents


Rats and mice damage wires, furniture, food products and can spread diseases.

Pest Identification

Identifying pests at an early stage is essential for preventing costly damage and health risks. Common signs include droppings, damaged wood, unusual sounds, bite marks, foul smells and visible pest activity.

Termites leave mud tubes and hollow wood. Cockroaches leave dark droppings and egg cases. Rodents create gnaw marks and nests. Mosquitoes are commonly found near stagnant water.

Damage Caused By Pests

Property Damage


Termites weaken wooden structures and rodents damage electrical wiring, increasing fire hazards.

Health Risks


Pests spread diseases and trigger allergies, asthma and skin irritations.

Food Contamination


Cockroaches and rodents contaminate food with bacteria and harmful pathogens.

Prevention Tips

  • ✔ Keep your surroundings clean and dry.
  • ✔ Store food in sealed containers.
  • ✔ Fix water leaks immediately.
  • ✔ Dispose of garbage regularly.
  • ✔ Seal cracks and entry points.
  • ✔ Schedule regular pest inspections.
  • ✔ Maintain proper ventilation.
  • ✔ Remove stagnant water around your property.
